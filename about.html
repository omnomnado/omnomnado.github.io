Hi! I’m Marta.
 I’m an illustrator and 2D animator based in Spain. I was born and raised in a city in southern Spain, Sevilla, and then I moved to Madrid to study a Film and Multimedia degree. Following that, I decided to finally take a 2D animation course which could be said to be my one “True love”.
At the moment I’m working on Cafelito Studios and part-timing as an art teacher.
I love cats, rainy days, comics and coffee, probably more than necessary.
