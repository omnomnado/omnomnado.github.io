CAFELITO STUDIO
A small but ambituous group of animators and artists based in Madrid, Spain. We do visual development as well as final art. Currently working on new projects and developing quality stories.
CHROMIC ZINES
Series of zines that recolects stories by various authors and artists. All inspired by one single colour that changes every edition.
JOJO'S BIZARRE FANZINE
A fanzine project based on JoJo's Bizarre Adventure by Hirohiko Araki. It's a collection of 35 illustrations by Spanish and latinamerican authors inspired by the first 5 arcs of the series. For all publics and with no spoilers.
